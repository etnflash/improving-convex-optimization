Why Diagnostics Matter

A single metric can be misleading. Robust optimization workflows track multiple signals: objective trend, constraint violation, stationarity, and gap-like criteria. This dashboard-style view helps identify stagnation, step-size mismatch, and feasibility issues early.

Core Metrics

  • Objective value \(f(x_k)\): are we making progress?
  • Feasibility residual: \(\|c(x_k)\|\) for constraints.
  • Stationarity proxy: \(\|\nabla f(x_k)\|\) or KKT residual.
  • Gap-style metric: primal-dual gap or surrogate stopping gap.

Checklist for Real Projects

  • Log all key metrics per iteration, not only objective.
  • Set separate tolerances for feasibility and stationarity.
  • Flag divergence early when objective drops but residuals explode.
  • Always inspect curves, not just final scalar summaries.

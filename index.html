Improving Convex Optimization

Learn convex optimization through clear intuition, visual examples, and accessible mathematics

Welcome

Welcome to our educational resource on convex optimization! This website is designed to help students, researchers, and practitioners understand the fundamental concepts of convex optimization through intuitive explanations, practical examples, and visual demonstrations.

Convex optimization is a powerful mathematical framework used in machine learning, signal processing, control systems, finance, and many other fields. Understanding its principles is essential for developing efficient algorithms and solving real-world problems.

Getting Started

If you're new to convex optimization, we recommend starting with the fundamentals:

  • Understanding Convexity - Learn what makes a set or function convex
  • Convex Functions - Explore the properties of convex functions
  • Strong Convexity & Smoothness - Relate curvature constants and condition numbers to convergence rates
  • Accelerated Gradient Methods - Compare GD and Nesterov momentum on ill-conditioned objectives
  • Subgradients & Optimality - Handle non-smooth convex models with first-order certificates
  • Jensen's Inequality - Connect convexity with expectation and averaging bounds
  • Conjugate Functions & Fenchel Duality - Derive dual forms and primal-dual insights from convex conjugates
  • KKT and Slater Conditions - Certify optimality and strong duality with constraint qualifications
  • Proximal Operators & Moreau Envelope - Solve non-smooth models with stable composite updates
  • Mirror Descent & Bregman Divergence - Use geometry-aware updates beyond Euclidean distance
  • ADMM - Split convex problems into efficient alternating subproblems
  • Primal-Dual Methods - Use PDHG/Chambolle-Pock style updates for structured saddle problems
  • Projected Gradient Descent - Optimize with constraints via projection-based updates
  • Line Search Methods - Pick robust step sizes with Armijo/Wolfe-style criteria
  • Projection Library for Convex Sets - Reusable projection formulas for box, ball, simplex, and affine constraints
  • Feasibility & Projection Algorithms - Compare POCS and Dykstra on intersections of convex sets
  • Interior-Point Methods - Follow central paths for high-accuracy constrained optimization
  • Conic Optimization - Unify LP/QP/SOCP/SDP formulations under cone constraints
  • Convergence Rates - Compare sublinear, accelerated, and linear convergence behaviors
  • Operator Splitting Methods - Build composite solvers from prox/gradient sub-operators
  • Solver Engineering Practice - Tune scaling, warm starts, and stopping rules for robust deployments
  • Stochastic Gradient Methods - Scale convex learning with unbiased noisy gradients
  • Variance Reduction Methods - Reduce SGD noise with SVRG/SAGA finite-sum corrections
  • Coordinate Descent - Optimize one variable block at a time for sparse high-dimensional models
  • Regularization Paths - Visualize how coefficients evolve as regularization changes
  • Optimization Diagnostics Dashboard - Track objective, feasibility, stationarity, and gap-style criteria
  • Convex Optimization Problems - Formulate and solve convex optimization problems
  • Algorithms & Duality - Connect solvers, certificates, and dual intuition
